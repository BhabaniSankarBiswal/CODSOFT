Frequently Asked Questions

Clear answers to details about logistics, eligibility, teams, and submissions.

InnovateX 2026 is open to college students (undergraduate & postgraduate), researchers, designers, early-stage startups, and developer enthusiasts nationwide. You must present valid college or corporate ID proofs at physical check-in.

Fill in the registration form in the Registration section above. Ensure you enter correct student emails and team sizes. Once registered, our community coordinators will send a confirmation link alongside the Discord channel invitations.

Teams can range from 1 member (Solo Developer) up to a maximum of 4 members. You can change team compositions or merge squads prior to the team validation round on Day 1.

No, there are zero participation or entry fees for any events. In addition, shortlisted finalist teams who qualify for the grand rounds in the hacker arena will be provided free boarding, lodging, and snacks on campus.

Teams are graded by a jury panel consisting of senior engineers, developers, and venture investors. Criteria include: (1) Technical complexity and code architecture, (2) User interface quality & responsiveness, (3) Novelty and product innovation, and (4) Value and business viability during the pitch deck presentation.

Cash rewards (Gold: ₹5L, Silver: ₹3L, Bronze: ₹2L) are wired directly to the designated bank accounts of the registered team leads within 14 business days of the closing ceremony. Trophy awards, cloud credits, and placement referral pathways are delivered immediately at the event.
